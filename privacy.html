Privacy

Privacy policy

What we collect, what we don't, and the choices you control.

The basics

MensGoldChains.com is an informational website. We don't require accounts and we don't ask for personal information to read our guides. We don't sell your personal data.

Analytics

We use Google Analytics 4 (GA4) to understand how visitors use the site — pages viewed, approximate location, device and browser type, and how people arrive. This data is largely aggregate and helps us improve our guides. Analytics run only after you accept cookies (see below).

Cookies & your choice

When you first visit, a banner lets you accept or decline analytics cookies. Analytics are off by default and only run if you accept — we use Google Consent Mode, so nothing analytics-related is stored until you opt in. Affiliate partners may also set cookies when you click an outbound link, so a purchase can be credited to us. You can block or clear cookies anytime in your browser settings.

Affiliate links

Some outbound links are affiliate links. When you click them, the destination retailer (such as Amazon or a jewelry retailer's network) may set a cookie to attribute a purchase. See our affiliate disclosure for details.

Contact form

If you message us through the contact page, we use the name and email you provide solely to respond to you.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Questions are welcome via the contact page.
