Karat, percentage, and fineness

These are three ways of saying the same thing — how much of the metal is gold:

KaratPurity %Fineness
10k41.7%417
14k58.3%585
18k75.0%750
22k91.7%916
24k99.9%999

Karat is out of 24; fineness is out of 1,000. To convert karat to fineness, divide by 24 and multiply by 1,000 (14 ÷ 24 × 1000 = 583, rounded to 585 by convention). Purity percentage is just the fineness as a percent.

Frequently asked questions

How do you convert karat to percentage?

Divide the karat by 24. So 14k is 14 ÷ 24 = 58.3% gold, 18k is 75%, and 10k is 41.7%. Fineness is the same figure out of 1,000 (585, 750, 417).

What does 585 fineness mean?

585 means 58.3% pure gold — the fineness stamp equivalent to 14k. 750 is 18k and 417 is 10k.

How much is a gram of 14k gold worth?

Roughly 58.3% of the pure-gold price per gram. Enter the current spot price above and the calculator gives the exact value of a gram of 14k at today's price.
