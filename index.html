The karat ledger

What the number on the clasp means

Every real gold chain is an alloy — pure gold is too soft to wear. The karat tells you how much of it is actually gold.

Full karat breakdown →
KaratGoldStampWhat it's for
10K41.7%417 / 416Hardest, most alloy. Budget chains and rough daily wear.
14K58.3%585The men's sweet spot — real gold color, daily durability, best US resale.
18K75.0%750Richer yellow, more gold value, but softer and pricier.
22K91.7%916Deep color, quite soft — uncommon for men's chains in the US.
24K99.9%999Pure gold. Too soft to hold a chain's shape.
